About Us

Fanshawe Free Will Baptist Church has been a cornerstone of our community since 1908.

We are a welcoming Christian congregation that loves God, our neighbors, and our town. Our church family is a vibrant mix of ages, with lots of children, active student ministries, and adults who serve with passion.

We also gather for Sunday morning breakfast and Wednesday evening supper—great opportunities for fellowship and building friendships. Whether you are new to faith or looking for a church home, we invite you to join us for worship, fellowship, and community service.
